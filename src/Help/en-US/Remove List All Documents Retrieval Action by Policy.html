Remove List All Documents Retrieval Action by Policy

In a multi-user environment, with a database that contains thousands of documents, it may be necessary to limit who can list all documents.

  • To remove List All Documents from the Document Retrieval list, apply SetRemoveListAllDocumentsPolicy.reg (included with PDFKeeper) to the targeted computers. Elevated privileges are required to apply this change.

  • To reverse the change, apply ClearRemoveListAllDocumentsPolicy.reg (included with PDFKeeper) to the targeted computers. Elevated privileges are required to apply this change.

Additional Information:

  • This policy is ignored when using the single-user database option.
